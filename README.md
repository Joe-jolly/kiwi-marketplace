# Kiwi Marketplace

Everything starts nearby.

---

## Overview

Kiwi is a location-based marketplace designed for migrants living in South Korea.

The platform helps users discover nearby items, services, jobs, housing, and opportunities within their local communities.

Kiwi focuses on a simple marketplace flow:

Browse

↓

Search

↓

Chat

↓

Deal

---

## Problem

Many migrants currently rely on:

* Telegram groups
* KakaoTalk chats
* Facebook groups
* Community forums

to buy, sell, and exchange information.

These channels are often:

* Unorganized
* Difficult to search
* Hard to trust
* Location-unaware

Kiwi aims to solve these problems through a dedicated local marketplace.

---

## Vision

Create the most trusted local discovery platform for migrants in South Korea.
