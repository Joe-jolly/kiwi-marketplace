# Kiwi Marketplace

## Brand Constitution

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

---

# 1. Brand Identity

Brand Name:

Kiwi

Tagline:

Everything starts nearby.

Category:

Location-Based Marketplace

Industry:

Marketplace Technology

Primary Market:

Migrants living in South Korea

---

# 2. Brand Mission

Kiwi helps migrants discover nearby items, services, jobs, housing, and opportunities through a simple and trustworthy marketplace experience.

The brand exists to reduce friction between people who need something and people who can provide it.

---

# 3. Brand Vision

Short-Term Vision:

Become the most useful marketplace for migrants in South Korea.

Mid-Term Vision:

Become the primary local discovery platform for migrant communities.

Long-Term Vision:

Enable people to discover opportunities around them, including:

* Products
* Services
* Jobs
* Housing
* Community Opportunities

Everything starts nearby.

---

# 4. Brand Positioning

Current Positioning:

Nearby Marketplace for Migrants

Future Positioning:

Local Discovery Platform

Positioning Statement:

Kiwi helps migrants discover nearby opportunities faster through a location-based marketplace designed for trust, simplicity, and local connection.

---

# 5. Brand Personality

Primary Traits:

* Friendly
* Helpful
* Trustworthy
* Local
* Simple

Secondary Traits:

* Modern
* Fast
* Clean
* Human

The brand should feel like:

A useful neighbor.

The brand should never feel like:

* A corporation
* A government service
* A social media company
* A luxury brand

---

# 6. Brand Values

Core Values:

* Trust
* Simplicity
* Accessibility
* Local Connection
* Community
* Practical Utility

Every design and product decision should support at least one of these values.

---

# 7. Brand Promise

Kiwi helps people discover nearby opportunities quickly and confidently.

Users should always feel:

* Safe
* In Control
* Connected
* Informed

---

# 8. Brand Voice

Communication Style:

* Simple
* Clear
* Friendly
* Helpful

Communication should be:

* Human
* Direct
* Respectful

Communication should avoid:

* Technical language
* Corporate jargon
* Aggressive marketing language

---

# 9. Visual Identity

Design Philosophy:

Marketplace First

Content First

Minimal Friction

Local First

Fast Navigation

Visual Style:

* Clean
* Modern
* Lightweight
* Friendly

The interface should prioritize usability over decoration.

# 14. Logo Constitution

Final Direction:

Kiwi Slice + Radar + Location Pin

Symbol Meaning:

Kiwi Slice

Represents community.

Seeds

Represent people.

Location Pin

Represents users.

Radar

Represents nearby opportunities.

Brand Message:

Everything starts nearby.

---

# 15. App Icon Constitution

Shape:

Circular

Core Symbol:

Kiwi Slice

Center Element:

Location Pin

Supporting Element:

Radar Sweep

Primary Color:

Kiwi Green

The icon must communicate:

* Nearby
* Discovery
* Marketplace
* Local Opportunities

Without requiring text.

---

# 16. UI/UX Alignment

Kiwi UI must align with the following principles:

* Marketplace First
* Content First
* Fast Navigation
* Minimal Friction
* Light Mode First
* Full Dark Mode Support

Inspired By:

Karrot Market

The application should prioritize user actions over visual effects.

---

# 17. Brand Expansion Rules

Future services may include:

* Services Marketplace
* Jobs Marketplace
* Housing Marketplace
* Community Discovery

All future products must remain consistent with the Kiwi identity.

---

# 18. Brand Consistency Rule

Whenever branding, UI, marketing, or product decisions conflict:

The following priority applies:

1. Trust
2. Simplicity
3. Local Connection
4. Accessibility
5. Visual Preference

Brand values always take priority over aesthetic preferences.
